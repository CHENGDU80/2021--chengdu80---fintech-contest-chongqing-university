Operation risk is the possibility that the future operating cash flow of an enterprise will change due to changes in production and operation or changes in market environment, thus affecting the market value of the enterprise.
How to prevent it:
1.Implement a Quality Assurance Program
2.Limit High-Risk Customers, implement a rule that customers with poor credit must pay ahead of time
3.Control Growth, train employees to focus on quality, not quantity
Legal risk refers to the risk caused by the invalidity of the contract within the scope of law and the inability to perform it, or the improper conclusion of the contract.
How to prevent it:
1.Strengthen risk awareness
2.Improve the work system. The establishment and improvement of legal risk prevention mechanism should be organically combined with speeding up the establishment of modern enterprise system and improving the corporate governance structure.
3.Speed up the construction of the enterprise legal adviser system with the general legal adviser system as the core.
Loan risk refers to the possibility of various losses faced by the lender in the process of operating the loan business, which is the measure to measure the degree of loan risk.
How to prevent it:
1.Strengthen the investigation and evaluation before the loan.
2.Determine a reasonable loan pledge rate.
3.Strict risk prevention measures should be stipulated in loan contract and pledge contract.
